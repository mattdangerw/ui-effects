ClutterPaintVolume

ClutterPaintVolume is an opaque structure whose members cannot be directly accessed. A ClutterPaintVolume represents an a bounding volume whose internal representation isn't defined but can be set and queried in terms of an axis aligned bounding box. A ClutterPaintVolume for a #ClutterActor is defined to be relative from the current actor modelview matrix. Other internal representation and methods for describing the bounding volume may be added in the future.
